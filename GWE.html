Our mission is to educate and inform women and girls around the world about the impact of warped body image on society. We want everyone to feel comfortable in their own bodies.

what is body image? Body image is the subjective picture or mental image of one's own body.

Famous body positive influencers!:


This is Ashley Graham. She is a model known for being in the industry for those who classify as curvy. She isn't afraid of calling out modeling industries who exclude certain groups of people and has been an insperation for many. She has also tried to make stretch marks become more normalized in society. In 2016 she made the cover of Sports Illustrated and hopes the cover will inspire women to love their own body. In an interview wth Elle Ashley said,"I think there are too many women who are self-conscious about the way they look, the way they see themselves in the mirror. I hope that this cover is a testament to them, proving to them that they are beautiful no matter what."

Ashley's Instagram



This is Megan Rose Lane. She is a beauty blogger, entrepreneur, self-love and mental health advocate who talks about her struggles with confidence and body image after giving birth. By using her story and impowering images and videos she hopes to build confidence in other young women. Megan uses her platform to inform and support those who have gone through the same expieriences as her such as eating disorders and mental health issues. accourding to female naratives Megan said"I realise that this world of ego, comparison, shallow materialistic insufficiency is suffocating me and deep down I KNOW happiness cannot be bought, earned or worked toward. Happiness is not 10lbs from now, it's not when you get your dream job, or when you're prettier, richer or more popular. Happiness is NOW. So stop fearing what isn't real and decide to live fully in the moment, fully in love with who you are on a level that's so much deeper than your eyebrows, achievements or shoe collection."
